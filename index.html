Project 01

2kW PMSM Motor Drive

A 2 kW PMSM drive developed for humanoid robot joint actuators, integrating an absolute magnetic encoder and supporting precise position, impedance, velocity, and torque control. The drive receives commands over CAN and includes overcurrent, overvoltage, and stall/locked-rotor protection for safe operation.

Project 02

Humanoid Robot Interface Board

A dual-STM32F7 interface board designed as the central communication hub of a humanoid robot. It links a Raspberry Pi master to distributed joint motor drives through eight independent CAN buses while supporting a 1 kHz real-time command cycle. The design integrates isolated power domains, high-speed SPI, RS-485/422, UART, I²C, voltage monitoring, ESD protection, and robust signal isolation for reliable operation in a noisy, high-power robotic system.

Project 03

Humanoid Power Distribution Board

A 48 V power distribution board developed for a humanoid robot, delivering high-current power to multiple independently controlled joint-actuator clusters. Each output integrates controlled precharge, high-side MOSFET switching, current sensing, voltage monitoring, isolated gate drive, and regenerative-energy protection. The architecture enables selective power-up, fault detection, and rapid shutdown of individual clusters, improving system safety, limiting inrush current, and preventing a failure in one section from disrupting the entire robot.

About

My work connects power electronics, embedded systems, and control.

I hold a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Tehran, with a focus on control and embedded systems. My work combines power electronics, PCB design, real-time firmware, communication networks, motor control, and learning-based methods for robotic applications.

I enjoy taking a system from its initial requirements to a working prototype: defining the architecture, selecting components, designing the hardware, developing the embedded control software, integrating the subsystems, and testing the final design under real operating conditions.
